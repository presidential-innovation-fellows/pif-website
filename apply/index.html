Joining Presidential Innovation Fellows (PIF)

Thank you for your interest in the Presidential Innovation Fellows (PIF) program! We're excited that you're excited to jump into public service with confidence. At PIF, you'll never go it alone. As a cohort, PIF's community and collective knowledge are here to support every fellow. Are you excited about the opportunity to create profound impact without sacrificing your passions? Email pif-application@pif.gov with the subject line "PIF interest" to be notified when our applications open.

Application timeline: PIF applications are currently closed. We typically accept applications annually from February through May, interview select candidates on a rolling basis May through July, and extend offers in late summer for an October start date.

Who may apply: All United States citizens and nationals (residents of American Samoa and Swains Islands) are eligible to apply. Non-U.S. citizens, current federal employees, or GSA contractors are not eligible to apply.

You can always learn more about our newest PIF cohort, current Presidential Innovation Fellows, read about our projects with federal partners, and connect with us on social media! You can also learn more about the federal hiring process on the Join TTS website.
